🥏 HuckHub
Madison's Ultimate Throwing Partner Finder

Connecting Madison's Ultimate Community for Better Throwing Skills

HuckHub is a Progressive Web App designed specifically for Madison's ultimate frisbee community to help players find throwing partners, improve their skills, and strengthen connections within our sport.

Alignment with MUFA's Mission

"To serve the Madison area Ultimate community, representing the interests of the sport and players. To maintain the Spirit of the Game as the central governing principal of the sport. To promote the growth and the development of the sport of Ultimate."

HuckHub directly supports MUFA's mission by:

  • Serving the Community: Providing a free tool that benefits all Madison ultimate players
  • Promoting Growth: Helping players develop skills between league seasons
  • Spirit of the Game: Encouraging positive, respectful interactions through safety features
  • Development: Creating opportunities for skill improvement and community building

Key Features

📍 Location-Based Matching

Find throwing partners based on your location and preferred Madison parks. Includes both MUFA venues and local parks.

🎯 Skill Level Compatibility

Match with players of similar skill levels (1-10 scale) for productive practice sessions that benefit both players.

🏆 League Level Preferences

Specify your league experience: MUFA, college/club, recreational, or beginner to find compatible partners.

⏰ Availability Matching

Set your weekly availability and find partners who are free during the same time slots.

📱 Progressive Web App

Works on any device - installs like a native app but accessible through any web browser.

🔄 Real-Time Updates

Get instant notifications when someone wants to throw with you, with email and push notifications.

Benefits for MUFA Members

  • Skill Development: Consistent throwing practice improves game performance
  • Community Building: Connect with players outside your usual league teams
  • New Player Support: Help newer players find mentors and practice partners
  • Off-Season Engagement: Keep players active between MUFA league seasons
  • Park Discovery: Learn about new throwing locations in Madison
  • Flexible Scheduling: Find partners for impromptu throwing sessions

🛡️ Safety & Community Standards

HuckHub prioritizes user safety and maintains high community standards:

Age Verification

All users must be 18+ with age verification during signup

User Reporting

Easy reporting system for inappropriate behavior with admin review

Blocking System

Users can block others and they won't appear in future matches

Safety Guidelines

Clear guidelines for safe meetings in public parks during daylight

Admin Moderation

Active moderation with ability to ban users who violate community standards

Privacy Protection

No tracking, minimal data collection, and clear privacy policy

Safety Guidelines for Users

  • Meet in public parks during daylight hours
  • Tell someone where you're going
  • Trust your instincts - if something feels wrong, leave
  • Report any inappropriate behavior immediately
  • Users meet at their own risk (clearly stated in terms)

Technical Specifications

Platform & Accessibility

  • Progressive Web App: Works on iOS, Android, and desktop browsers
  • No App Store Required: Install directly from web browser
  • Offline Capable: Basic functionality works without internet
  • Responsive Design: Optimized for mobile and desktop use

Data & Privacy

  • Minimal Data Collection: Only essential information for matching
  • No Tracking: No analytics, cookies, or third-party tracking
  • Secure Storage: Data encrypted in transit and at rest via Supabase
  • User Control: Users can delete their accounts and data at any time

How It Works

  1. Sign Up: Create account with email verification and age confirmation
  2. Complete Profile: Set skill level, league experience, availability, and preferred parks
  3. Find Partners: Browse available players or start seeking to be found
  4. Connect: Send throwing requests with optional personal messages
  5. Meet & Throw: Coordinate meeting times and locations
  6. Build Community: Develop ongoing throwing relationships
